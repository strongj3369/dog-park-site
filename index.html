  • Island Dog Park

    This dog park features a much larger fenced-in area with more than enough room to roam. The terrains are well managed, with easy passage to both restrooms and parking. Table accommodation is provided for owners who will appreciate a day with their most loyal friend in tow.

  • Memorial Dog Park

    A dedicated area for large and small dogs ensures your pup won’t be mingling out of their weight class. Your pup will love the many obstacles they have to navigate while enjoying the fresh bay-front air. The park has plenty of picnic seating for owners with nearby bathrooms and ample parking.

  • Rolling Green Fields

    This park features a large area with lots of room for your dog to burn off some energy—plenty of sprawling green grass for the off-leash pet areas. The is a beach across the street is the perfect spot to cool down! Your pups will be found splashing along the shore while their owners soak up the sun; it is an excellent place for the whole family.
